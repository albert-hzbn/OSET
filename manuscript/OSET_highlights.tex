\item Dislocations emerge as a collective, large-N limit of OSTZ chains.
\item Peierls stress, core energy, and stacking-fault energy are derived, not fitted.
\item Validated against grain-boundary-sliding data across metals, glasses, ceramics.
\item Unifies crystal and lattice-free plasticity within Eshelby's inclusion theory.
